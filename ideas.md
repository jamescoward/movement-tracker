# Chart & Visualisation Ideas

Ideas for future charts and data views, beyond the core daily chart and heat map.

## Timeline & Pattern Charts

- **Hourly distribution curve** — bell-curve style showing which hours of the day are most active, overlaid across multiple days
- **Movement streak chart** — highlight consecutive hours with/without movement to spot quiet and active windows
- **Day-over-day overlay** — stack multiple days' timelines on top of each other (semi-transparent) to see pattern convergence
- **Rolling 7-day average** — line chart of daily totals with a smoothed rolling average to show trends

## Flag Correlation Charts

- **Flag breakdown pie/donut** — what percentage of movements had each flag active
- **Flag vs. time heatmap** — do flagged movements cluster at certain times? (e.g. post-meal kicks always at 7pm)
- **Before/after flag comparison** — movement frequency in the hour before vs. after eating, resting, etc.
- **Flag combo analysis** — which flag combinations produce the most/least movement

## Summary & Reporting

- **Weekly report card** — total movements, most active day, quietest day, most common flag, baseline comparison
- **Calendar view** — month grid with colour-coded cells (green = above baseline, amber = below, grey = no data)
- **Personal records** — most movements in a day, longest active window, longest quiet window
- **Gestational age overlay** — if due date is entered, show movement patterns mapped to weeks of pregnancy

## Comparison & Trend Charts

- **Week-over-week comparison** — side-by-side bar charts of this week vs. last week
- **Baseline drift** — how is the baseline itself changing over weeks? Is baby getting more or less active over time?
- **Time-to-10 chart** — track how long it takes to reach 10 movements each day (a common clinical benchmark)

## Ambient / Glanceable

- **Today's progress ring** — circular progress indicator showing movements today vs. baseline expectation
- **Live ticker** — minimal view showing time since last movement and count today
- **Widget-ready summary** — a compact data card suitable for a PWA widget or home screen glance
